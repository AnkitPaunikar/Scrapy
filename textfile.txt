--location: (required) The location where you want to find jobs.
--experience:(required) The number of years of experience you want the job to require.
--roles:(required) A space-separated list of job titles to search for.
--freshness: (Optional) Number of days to filter jobs by freshness. Default is 7 days.
--timeout: (Optional) Time limit for the script to run in minutes. Default is 320 minutes.
